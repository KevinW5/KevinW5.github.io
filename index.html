Foods You Need to Try

Stir Fry Pig Blood

Stir-fried pig blood is a traditional Chinese dish known for its unique texture and health benefits. It's often cooked with garlic, ginger, and green onions for a tasty, savory flavor. This dish shows how Chinese cuisine uses all parts of an animal.

Bunny Chow

Bunny chow is a popular South African street food that originated in the Indian community of Durban. It consists of a hollowed-out loaf of bread filled with spicy curry, usually made with chicken, lamb, or vegetables. This dish is enjoyed for its flavorful and hearty combination of bread and curry.

Apfelstrudel

Apfelstrudel is a traditional Austrian pastry known for its thin, flaky dough and sweet apple filling. The filling typically includes apples, raisins, sugar, cinnamon, and breadcrumbs, creating a delicious blend of flavors and textures. Often served warm with powdered sugar or vanilla sauce, Apfelstrudel is a beloved dessert in many Central European countries.
